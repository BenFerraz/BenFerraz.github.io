SHATTERLINE: OVERDRIVE

Shatterline: Overdrive

Arcade Racer | Unity | C# | Blender


Shatterline: Overdrive is a high-octane, arcade-style racing game inspired by the explosive action of Split/Second: Velocity. Built in Unity with C#, this university project received a solid 77/100, highlighting both its creative ambition and technical execution.

I handled every aspect of development, including scripting dynamic gameplay systems and crafting all 3D models and textures using Blender. The game is a testament to my ability to merge fast-paced gameplay with striking visuals, while demonstrating a strong foundation in end-to-end game development and asset creation.
